General Info
The Corpus Christi is an ancient celebration that is part of a religious festival that dates back to the years of colonization in Panama, has a folkloric character with dances and local customs, that you will love.
Activities
See and also see how to learn the traditional dance its steps like: the Great Devil of La Villa de Los Santos.

Visit the churches.

Participate in processions in the streets and public places in which the body of Christ is exhibited and accompanied by crowds of the faithful.
