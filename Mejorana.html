General Info
The mejorana festival is a wonderful event that is celebrated in the town of Los Santos, it is characterized by a large number of parades, which have several delegations. In the parade people show the dances and musical expressions of Panama.
Activities
Play mejoranera
People have the oportunity to learn how to play simple notes of the mejoranera.

Hierra activity
You can safely enjoy the professionals who ride blind bulls and cows.
